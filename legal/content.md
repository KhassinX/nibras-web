# Content & Rights

Nibras presents the Arabic Qur'an together with translations of its meaning. The Arabic text is reproduced verbatim and unchanged; each translation of the meaning is labelled as such and attributed to its translator and publisher, in the app and on our [Legal](/legal/) page. We take the rights of translators and publishers seriously — and we are always glad to welcome new, freely-licensed translations of the meaning.

## Requesting removal or review of content {#removal}

If you are a rights-holder, an authority, or anyone who believes that content in Nibras should be removed or reviewed — whether on copyright grounds or to comply with a legal requirement — you are welcome to write to us. We review every request in good faith and respond promptly.

Please email **[legal@khassinx.com](mailto:legal@khassinx.com)** with:

- **The specific content** — the edition or translation of the meaning, the sura, and the ayah, with enough detail for us to locate it.
- **The basis for your request** — the right you hold or the legal requirement you are asserting, and your authority to make the request.
- **How to reach you** for any follow-up.

We will acknowledge your message, review it, and reply. If content was included in error, or if we are required to remove it, we will do so. Where a request is unclear or appears mistaken, we will explain our understanding and continue to work with you in good faith.

Nibras is operated by KHASSINX LLC, a Florida limited liability company; these matters are governed by the laws of the State of Florida. This page is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.

## Contribute a translation of the meaning {#contribute}

The Arabic Qur'an remains exactly as it is; what can grow is the set of translations of its meaning. If you know of a translation of the meaning of the Qur'an offered under a free license, or that its publisher would allow us to include — as QuranEnc kindly permitted — we would be honored to add it and to credit the translator and publisher in full. We can only include translations that are free to share or that we have clear written permission to use.

To suggest a translation of the meaning, write to **[community@khassinx.com](mailto:community@khassinx.com)**. Any edition we add is credited to its translator and publisher, in the app and on this site.
